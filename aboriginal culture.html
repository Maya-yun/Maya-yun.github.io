Aboriginal culture of CHI competition

In the context of multiculturalism, the protection of indigenous culture should focus on the inheritance of the spiritual level and the inheritance of the material level. Our hardware functions are mainly to showcase indigenous cultural products and man-machine interaction. The software app has four main sections: Indigenous Planet, Indigenous Community, Game Interaction, and Personal Homepage. The original intention is to improve the efficiency of spreading Indigenous culture through a more cartoon character form. On the whole, our design process is quite successful. Everyone is actively involved in this topic and has put forward a lot of practical suggestions for us.

The first idea we discussed was that the appearance of the hardware was made into a square device like a beverage vending machine, and the appearance of the device was designed to draw colorful indigenous elements through graffiti so as to attract people to stop and watch. In the device, the items are arranged line by line. Each item has a corresponding QR code. People can use the QR code to get the items they like for free.

When scanning the QR code, the same on the phone. Entering our software interface, the software interface starts with a rotating 3D globe. Through the sliding of fingers, we can see the icons of different indigenous tribes, click in, and the public will visit some famous buildings and indigenous special products as a tourist. And there will be narration to introduce different places, which will not only have interactive effects, but also have the function of spreading indigenous culture. This idea is based on some foreign charitable creative designs, such as card slides, and the screen will have corresponding interactions.
