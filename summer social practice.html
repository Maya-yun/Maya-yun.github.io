Summer social practice

he tenth party congress in order to thoroughly study and apply the school on the development of the high quality, leading the education the broad masses of young students to feel the 70th anniversary of the founding of new China made great achievements, make the students to be more vigorous state into the great socialism construction, industrial design institute majoring in teachers and students around the party to build and carry out the "youth with responsibility for student party members" summer social practice.

On the morning of August 5, the practice group came to lens technology group to visit and study. Lens technology group is an important strategic partner of OPPO. "Paper to come to eventually feel shallow, must know this matter to practice." On the road to knowledge, we should not only "read ten thousand books" but also "travel ten thousand miles". Teachers and students followed the staff to the production workshop for a visit, we have a more detailed understanding of the production process, at the same time to learn the early theoretical knowledge and practice of organic combination, benefit.

Through the visit and study in lens science and technology group and OPPO communication company, students learned about the operation mode, product design process and knowledge and skills required by industrial product design companies, deeply realized the future development direction of their major, and improved their cognition of industrial products.
